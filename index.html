College of Engineering

Future Engineers, get ready to be inspired!

APPRECIATE is a two-day research presentation and awards program that aims to advance the understanding of higher education, particularly in the field of engineering. The event features different research sessions, including Plenary Lectures, Parallel Research Presentations, and Project Booths and Exhibits. Various guests and speakers from different fields and industries will be invited to provide career-building talks for COE students.
